Lasagna Recipe

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that you can easily make at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 Olive oil
  • 750g Lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • 800g passata
  • 200ml Hot beef stock
  • Nutmeg
  • 300g fresh lasagne sheets
  • White Sauce
  • 125g Ball Mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan and fry the mince until browned.
  2. Add the prosciutto and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Stir in the passata and beef stock, then season with nutmeg.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes until thickened.
  5. Layer the lasagne sheets with the meat sauce and white sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Top with mozzarella and bake in a preheated oven at 180Â°C for 30-35 minutes.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your meal!
